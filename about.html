About us

We are BusyJollof. An online food ordering and delivery service. Yes, you read that right! "Another food delivery service, yet again" As you might have figured out, the food delivery market is already saturated by a lot of heavy hitters controlling what when you can eat to how much any restaurant on their platforms makes. However, we are not here to compete with any of that.

That's why we are coming into the scene as the solution to your favorite long-sorted African meal service provider serving various meals and meal categories the other big guys do not. To our hungry customers, our platform will help you discover and deliver fresh, authentic African-inspired meals. To our restaurant partners, our platform will present you with a new way to reach your targeted customer base.

BusyJollof is on a mission to change the African food ordering and delivery experience, and we will not stop until we achieve that.
